Bio

I'm an endlessly curious knowledge-junkie that's always learning, growing, & building. As a founder, I'm used to tackling problems head on and love moving fast. I'm passionate about solving meaningful, complex, high-impact problems. I love design thinking and the creative process, for their ability to push the human race forward. That's why in an effort to combat Climate Change, I spent the last four years building, selling, and designing electric vehicles.
